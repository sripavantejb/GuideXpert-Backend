# Counselling Quotas

## What is home state quota?

Home state quota reserves a portion of seats in NITs for candidates whose home state matches the NIT's location. For example, an NIT in a given state may have separate seat pools for home-state and other-state candidates.

## What is other state quota?

Other state quota refers to seats in an NIT allocated to candidates from states other than the NIT's home state. Cutoffs and competition can differ between home state and other state quotas for the same branch.

## What are category quotas in JEE counselling?

Category quotas include seats reserved for GEN-EWS, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, and PwD candidates as per government rules. Each category has its own merit list and seat allocation rules during JoSAA counselling.

## What is the female supernumerary quota?

Some institutes may offer supernumerary seats for female candidates in certain branches as per official policy for that year. Details are published in the JoSAA business rules and institute-specific notices.

## What is PwD quota?

PwD (Persons with Disabilities) quota provides reserved seats for eligible candidates with specified disabilities, subject to certificate requirements and horizontal reservation rules defined in the official counselling brochure.
